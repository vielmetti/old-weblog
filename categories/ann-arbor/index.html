Reduce your water bill in four (well, five) easy steps

Identify the toilet tank in the basement that no one uses that's leaking. Squirt WD-40 on the stuck valve on the supply line to the tank. Use elbow grease and a vise-grip to turn the supply line valve to "off". Remind people not to use the toilet. Check your daily usage to see that instead of using 200 units of water on a slow day, you're using 50 (!). I don't want to guess how much water (and money) was leaking down the drain, but I think I have this problem fixed for now. »
