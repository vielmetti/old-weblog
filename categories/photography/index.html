Yahoo makes changes to Flickr, users are not happy

The site loads slowly, the photos are all squished together, and the carefully curated metadata and tags and comments and photo names are de-emphasized. It's like a weird mix of Pinterest and Tumblr. I'm hoping for a classic.flickr.com to preserve the old UI, in the same way that old-school Weather Underground users can use classic.wunderground.com to see things the way they were meant to be. If I had the time, I'd cherry pick the best photos from my Flickr stream and blog about them here. »
