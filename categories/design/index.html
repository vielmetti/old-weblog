hearts, spades, clubs, diamonds, and bots

Pete Kaminski pointed me to this essay on archetypes in computer gaming, in which four types of play style are described. Hearts play for social interaction, spades play to explore, diamonds play to level up, and clubs play to create havoc. It is a nice description of state of the art online game play in the MUD and MOO era of online gaming. What has changed a lot since those olden days is the emergence of a new class of player, one who builds and operates bots to amplify their own game play. »
