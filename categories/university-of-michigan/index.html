500+ University of Michigan faculty protest "Administrative Services Transformation" project in open letter

"An Open Letter to President Coleman and Provost Pollack", published on um-openletter.eecs.umich.edu/ - from the lead paragraph: We, the undersigned faculty of the University of Michigan, support your intent to realign administrative expenses relative to revenues. However we collectively reject the proposed Administrative Services Transformation (AST) project. We respectfully offer an alternative approach to AST, which we call Unit- Centric Services (UCS). We believe that the AST approach is inherently flawed because its focus is on reducing administrative costs without taking into account the concurrent reduction in faculty and staff productivity, collaborative academic culture, and the unique needs of heterogeneous academic units. »
