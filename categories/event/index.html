Paw Paws at Cobblestone Farm Market on Tuesday, 22 October 2013

The following was posted to the Cobblestone Farm mailing list and is repeated here for your edification (and enjoyment of distinctive fruit). Cobblestone Market has much to offer you and your family this Tuesday evening (4pm-7pm). Come try some new tastes, then warm your hands and feet by our fire pit!   There will be paw paws at the market tomorrow, thanks to Marc Boone and his volunteers! Paw paws are a native fruit which tastes a like a banana and mango combined.  Come have a taste and take some home. »
