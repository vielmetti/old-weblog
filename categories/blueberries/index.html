Early July berry picking report, 2011

U-Pick Strawberry season in the Ann Arbor area is over; it ended June 29 at Rowe's, and June 26 at Whittaker's. Juneberries are still on the trees, but they are overripe and best left for the birds. Mulberries are out in full force on the trees and on the sidewalks. Look down for black blotches, then look up for ripe berries. Blackberries and black raspberries can be found in parks or in the yards of helpful neighbors. »
