Obituary: David E. Davis Jr., 80; dean of automotive journalists

David E. Davis, Jr. (b. November 7, 1930, Burnside, Kentucky; d. March 27, 2011, Ann Arbor, Michigan) was the founder of Automobile Magazine and a long time Car and Driver editor. His distinctive voice defined modern automotive journalism. He is survived by a wife, Jeannie, a daughter, and two sons, many friends, and a generation of automotive writers and car lovers. Services are pending. Memorials: Automobile Magazine: Davis was a lifelong car enthusiast who worked in all corners of the industry, from racing to advertising to editorial and publishing. »
