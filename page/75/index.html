quake lakes and Dave's Landslide Blog

After an earthquake, landslides block river valleys, causing temporary lakes to form. Sometimes these lakes hold for a long time; in other cases, the earthen dam fails, causing flooding downstream. The single best blog on the topic is Dave's Landslide Blog, from Dave Petley, who is the Wilson Professor at Durham University, UK.
