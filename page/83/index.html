Neighborhood resources vs. cash resources

I’ve had good experiences with sharing of surplus resources at a neighborhood level. The folks on my block regularly borrow ladders, save up canning jars, pick each others surplus tomatoes, take care of cats etc as needed. Sometimes token amounts of cash change hands - mostly to pay kids - but often not. I look at the kinds of businesses and business plans aimed at monetizing neighborliness with a bit of skepticism. »
