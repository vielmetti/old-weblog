Github-based kanban boards: Zenhub, Waffle, Huboard, Blossom et al

A kanban board is simply a two-dimensional view of a series of work queues. By moving a piece of work from one column to another, you update its status. This gives you a quick way to visualize your current priorities, as well as making it very clear when one or more of your queues are too full. I've been using Github issues as a personal issue tracker for a while, using its milestones feature to track categories. »
