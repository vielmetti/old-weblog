2013 Moore Oklahoma tornado path map

This map from the National Weather Service in Norman, Oklahoma (via Atlantic Cities) shows the destructive path of the Moore, Oklahoma tornado of 20 May 2013. This is a preliminary map that also shows the track of a 3 May 1999 tornado that followed a very similar path. There's too much graphic, devastating imagery from the scene which I won't include here. Dr Jeff Masters has an excellent overview of the meteorology of the event, including accounts of debris being thrown 100 miles in the air to land on Tulsa, Oklahoma. »

New map of the dark side of the moon from the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter Camera (LROC)

The LROC (Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter Camera) orbits the moon, taking high resolution photos and beaming back data for analysis. This photo shows relief of the far side (the "dark side") of the moon, with false color indicating terrain. LROC WAC color shaded relief of the lunar farside (NASA/GSFC/DLR/Arizona State University). »

Edward Vielmetti on #moon, #maps, 20 Nov 2011

Black River Falls near Ishpeming Michigan - fire maps and links

Google search: black river falls michigan fire map Twitter search: black river falls As of 21 May 2009 there is a fire burning near Ishpeming, MI called the Black River Falls fire.  It's not in a densely populated area, but several dozen structures have burned, and there's a lot of crews that have been brought in to help fight the fire. When the Sleeper Lake Fire burned near Newberry, MI there was a lot of news that came out piecemeal from media, residents, and bloggers in the area.  I'm hoping to collect some of that same level of detail here.  It's always a big concern when forests burn, especially when they burn uncontrolled in land that doesn't have a lot of roads in it. »
