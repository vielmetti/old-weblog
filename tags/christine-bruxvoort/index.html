10/4 4-5p FLY Art Center volunteer orientation, FLY Creativity Lab, Ypsilanti

Christine Bruxvoort sent me these details on the FLY Art Center's upcoming volunteer orientation. When the volunteer orientation is going to be: Oct 4, 2014 4:00 p.m. - 5:00 p.m. Where it's going to be: FLY Creativity Lab, 40 N. Huron, Ypsilanti, 48197 (between Michigan and Pearl). Contact: Chris at 734-217-2228 or info@flyartcenter.org. Who is invited: Any interested adult or teen. Come to find out what we are about or sign up that night on our volunteer list. »
