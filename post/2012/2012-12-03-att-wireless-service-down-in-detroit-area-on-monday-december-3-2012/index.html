AT&T wireless service down in Detroit area on Monday, December 3, 2012

Multiple media sources are reporting AT&T downtime on their wireless network. Data and text messages are said to be unaffected. No word yet what the cause of the outage is or the estimated time of repair.

News sources:

AnnArbor.com, AT&T Wireless problems affecting calls in Ann Arbor. 

WXYZ: AT&T outage affecting metro Detroit customers

AT&T community forums: Metro Detroit South East Michigan Outage

Update: 10:29 a.m. Reports from the community forum are that service is coming back.
