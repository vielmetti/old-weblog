espresso over ice with a little half and half

A simple recipe that you can order from your favorite barista: an espresso shot over ice. Leave enough room to add a spot of half and half. Cold, refreshing, and very strong.

To try: a shot of espresso over shaved ice. I think this is doable at Ann Arbor Farmers Market where you’d go to the stall where a granny is selling shaved ice, and then walk a plain snow cone over to the Roos Roast stand for your espresso shot.

Inspired by a Facebook post from Allen Leibowitz in Texas ordering a snow cone and the surrounding commentary.
