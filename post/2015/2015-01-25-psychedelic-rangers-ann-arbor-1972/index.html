Psychedelic Rangers, Ann Arbor, 1972

The Ann Arbor Downtown Development Authority is considering hiring "downtown ambassadors" to patrol city streets, help out visitors, and keep the downtown neat and clean and welcoming.

As a counterpoint to this corporatized welcoming function, I give you the Psychedelic Rangers, a project of the Rainbow People's Party (1972) which provided security to concerts and generally kept the peace without bringing in the law.
