16 July 2015

a2b3 lunch at Ayse's, Art Fair 2015 edition

Eight of us met for lunch at Ayse’s today. It was a normal Thursday in that regard, as that’s a pretty typical size for the lunch group these days.

We talked at question time about how we get big chunks of time to work on projects. One answer was to work early in the morning or late at night when there were fewer distractions. Gyll, a remodeling contractor, mentioned the use of checklists to make sure that the things that needed to get done got done. Among the tasks that took up lots more time than they deserved included dealing with the health insurance marketplace and with other healthcare providers for dealing with billing and accounting problems.

Three of the people at lunch were new to the table, but since Ann Arbor is bascially a small town, it was not a surprise to me that I had easily identifiable mutual friends with two of them.

Food was delicious, well prepared and well presented.

Next week we’ll be back at Madras Masala, as art fair will have ended and town will be more back to normal.

The big event coming up is Maker Faire Detroit, to be held at The Henry Ford in Dearborn.
