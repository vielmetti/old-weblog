SpaceX CRS-7 launch failure

Jun 28, 2015

June 28, 2015. The SpaceX CRS-7 resupply mission to the International Space Station failed this morning.

The vehicle experienced an anomaly on ascent. Team is investigating. Updates to come.

— SpaceX (@SpaceX) June 28, 2015

Awful. pic.twitter.com/m0ediJGzeg #SpaceX @NASA via @MickiMaynard

— David Beard (@dabeard) June 28, 2015

More details live on NASA TV due at 12:30 p.m. EST, Sunday June 28, 2015. Wikipedia's SpaceX CRS-7 page is as good as you make of it.
