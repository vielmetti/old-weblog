10 December 2015

Thanks to everyone for birthday wishes, 2015 edition

Thanks to everyone for birthday wishes this year.

It is increasingly clear that the place to go for birthday greetings is Facebook, which is absurdly efficient both at providing “happy birthday” messages as well as acknowledging them.

Additional greetings came in via email, Skype, Twitter, and paper mail (hand written!, complete with reply postage!)

Lunch at a2b3 was a Thursday event, with 14 at the table at Blue Nile. The topic of the conversation was roughly “places to take a walk outside in the winter”, and the dog-owners among us amended to include “with a dog”, and we got a pretty good list that I want to get onto Arborwiki.

As always the Arborwiki birthday deals page came through, and I scored freebies or discounts at Northside Grill (breakfast sandwich), Roos Roast (mocha), Zingerman’s Bakery (bread) and Zingerman’s Coffee (coffee).

The technical commentary of the year is that Twitter has an opportunity to step up its birthday game, and it would seem that that’s a chance to do things right (or do things differently from Facebook, at least).

Until the next time around the sun!
