2015 Skywarn training

11 April 2015

2015 Skywarn training

After three or four years of planning to get Skywarn training, this year I finally did it. The good folks at Washtenaw County Emergency Operations Center and at the National Weather Service in White Lake, MI did a training session at Pioneer High School. The class was concurrent with a Michigan ballroom dancing competition so there was music and festivities and accomplished looking dancers to enjoy before the class.

I was there with John Hritz KD8MKE and Ann Hanson. Ann captured the opening slide. There was a nifty online multiplayer game (can't recall the site it was built on) which had 95 people playing along from their smart phones!

Skywarn Spotter Training in A2 now! Hanging w/ @vielmetti @jhritz Thanks @NWSDetroit pic.twitter.com/0X078Jl5Dg

— annyhanny (@annyhanny) April 11, 2015

My spotter ID good for 2 years is 242.

I brought my radio to the event and made a contact from the car with Marty KD8RGL from Canton MI. We were both using 5 watt radios contacting the W8UM repeater in Ann Arbor - I'm always surprised how well things work when they do work.

I discovered that it works a lot better to identify myself as "Eddie" when on the air. This because "Ed" gets misinterpreted as "Andy" all too often, and "Edward" seems awfully formal.
