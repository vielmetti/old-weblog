2015 May 4 Ann Arbor City Council preview, in map form.

Thanks in large part to the hard work of Dave Askins of Civ City, here's a preview map snapshot of the agenda for Ann Arbor City Council for May 4, 2015. Click through for detail which will take you to Arborwiki's accounts for each of those items tagged with the tag "20150504ccmtg".


This map reflects these agenda items:

  • 590 W. Ellsworth Road
  • African American Downtown Festival
  • Allmendinger Park
  • Ann Arbor Airport
  • Ann Arbor Book Festival
  • Ann Arbor Jaycees Fourth of July Parade: Routes
  • Arrowwood Hills Cooperative
  • Barton Sidewalk Project
  • Cinetopia: Maynard Street
  • Firecracker 5K
  • Fire Station 3
  • Flagstar Bank: 3600 Plymouth Road
  • Hands-On Museum
  • Hunt Park
  • Scio Church Sidewalk Project
  • Traver Street Storm Sewer Project

Knowing City Council, it's likely that one or more issues will emerge at the last possible moment, but this at least gives you a shot at figuring out what council actions are happening in your back yard.
