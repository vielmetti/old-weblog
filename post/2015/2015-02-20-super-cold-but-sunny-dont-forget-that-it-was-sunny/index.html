super cold (but sunny; don't forget that it was sunny)

Feb 20, 2015

One of my favorite weather forecasts comes from forecast.io. This screen shows the ensemble set of forecasts they have for temperature for tomorrow (February 20, 2015), highlighting the 8:00 a.m. reading.


You'll note from this graph that there are several weather models they are pulling from. The one of them that has the coldest predicted weather is RAP from the National Center for Atmospheric Research in Boulder, Colorado. Looking at their set of pages they have a nice cold temperature forecast map. Brrr! You have to head pretty far to escape the frigid air, it looks like the vector towards warmth is to the southwest


Your mileage (and temperature) may vary from the predictions.
