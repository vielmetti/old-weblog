2015 Graduation Day Galesburg MI earthquake rattles Ann Arbor, Grand Rapids, Detroit on May 2

May 2, 2015, ANN ARBOR, MICHIGAN: A magnitude 4.0 earthquake rattled southern Michigan at 12:23 p.m. today, with the quake centered at Galesburg, MI.

Timeline

May 2, 2015, 12:59 p.m. EST: Official report gives the quake ID us20002avh. "Did you feel it" map shows the range of the affected area.



May 2, 2015, 12:37 p.m. EST: No official report yet from USGS, but an earthquake rattled the southern half of lower Michigan at about 12:25 p.m. No reports of any damage or injuries, just mild shaking. Reports are in from Holland, Grand Rapids, Lansing area, Ann Arbor, Ypsilanti, Detroit, and one report from somewhere in Ohio. Hashtag #miquake . To report a quake to USGS see their Tell Us! page
