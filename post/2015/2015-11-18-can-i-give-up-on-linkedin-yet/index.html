Can I give up on LinkedIn yet?

Nov 18, 2015

I’m thinking about deleting my LinkedIn account.

I was an early adopter of LinkedIn back when it first got started. It was interesting, fun, cool to collect all your friends.

Now, my sense is that my LinkedIn profile is useless at best and actively unhelpful at worst. Why would I want to publish a standardized autobiography of work experience in a medium that doesn’t reward complicated life histories? It’s not helpful - what’s more, it’s not clear that it’s ever been helpful for the narrowly defined task of getting work.

I’m interested in hearing from you if you have deleted your LinkedIn (or have stayed away from it).

Edward Vielmetti
