17 June 2015

Golden Rose Little Free Library now open

A new Little Free Library is now open at the corner of Golden and Rose in Ann Arbor.


From the owner:

We hope that you'll stop by to share books that you have enjoyed reading and take a book that inspires you. Feel free to leave a note inside of the book for the next lucky neighbor telling them what you liked about the book. We hope to keep it stocked with books for kids, teens, and adults!

For more book boxes in town see the Little Free Libraries page on Arborwiki, which has a nice map.
