long form

Aug 9, 2015

Too much of the stuff that I write is very short. I’d like to sort out how to have more long form writing.

From memories of times past trying to do this, the struggle was in coming up with structure. It’s easy to push out a 5 paragraph essay in comparison with structuring a 20 or 50 or 200 page tome.

I know that people have taken blogs as first drafts of long form writing, and heaven knows I have enough raw word count to start to make something from.
