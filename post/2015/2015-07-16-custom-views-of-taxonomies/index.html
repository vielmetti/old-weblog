custom views of taxonomies

One of the motivation ideas behind going to the work to move a big weblog to a new platform is to get better control over categories. With Typepad, every category looks about the same; the idea now is with Hugo I can have separate categories that look different. For instance:

There are a number of recipes in the collection, which should be sorted alphabetically or by key ingredient, not chronologically.

There are a number of posts about books in the collection, which should be connected to a lookup for that book in your favorite bookstore or my favorite library.

There are a number of people mentioned, and a people index would be great so you can browse and not just search.

Mostly I’m after improving the browsing experience, as much for me as for anyone else. If you have to find things with a search engine, you are stuck in a world where you have to know that something has a particular name. By offering a browsing infrastructure first, you cherrypick the best search terms and put them up front.

Some of this will need pen and paper design….
