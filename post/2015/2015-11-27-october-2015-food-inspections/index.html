October 2015 food inspections for Washtenaw County

Every month or so the Washtenaw County Health Department releases the previous month’s health inspection reports. These are published to the county’s contracted web service, and a copy of those is extracted for the Food Inspections service designed by Wayne Eaker.

There are 272 inspections for October 2015 and 50 more for November 2015 available. Here are some of the low lights of this month’s round of inspections, places where you might want to think about twice before making a visit.

Dalat, Ypsilanti, inspected on November 3. “Observed sewage backing up to the floor underneath the food prep sink.” “Raw chicken and beef boxes being used to store other ready to eat foods (i.e. vegtables) in various coolers in the facility.” 12 violations total, 8 critical.

Melting Pot, Ann Arbor, their last inspection on November 2 before the restaurant closed for good later in the month. “Observed the dishwasher go from loading soiled dishes to putting away clean dishes without washing hands.”

Great Plains Burger Company, Ann Arbor, inspected on October 21 with 14 total violations. “Observed many small flies (fruit flies) around prep sink where french fries are prepared.” “Containers used to store french fries observed soiled. A cleaned inverted container observed with many flies trapped under it and were seen landing on container.”

Shalimar Restaraunt on Main Street in Ann Arbor, inspected on October 29 with 3 total violations. “Observed 4 bottles of liquor with flies inside.”

Haab’s Restaurant in Ypsilanti with 15 violations on October 21. “Observed the hand sink for the basement kitchen wrapped in plastic wrap to avoid using due to unrepair leaking. The only available hand sink is in another area through a door and significant distance away from this kitchen.” “Observed severe infestation of drain flies noted at bar and main kitchen.” “Observed drain flies in the bottom of open bottle of scotch at bar.”

Please check out your favorite destination before visiting!
