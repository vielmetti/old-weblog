Boston Marathon, 2013

My first reaction to the horrible events of today:

  • Don't look at pictures, especially not the graphic ones. There isn't time to react.
  • Focus on something small, like a neighbor who was running the marathon who is ok.
  • Mistrust many news sources, because some people will write anything to get attention.

The Wikipedia page is pretty reasonable for coverage, though from time to time nonsense sneaks in.
