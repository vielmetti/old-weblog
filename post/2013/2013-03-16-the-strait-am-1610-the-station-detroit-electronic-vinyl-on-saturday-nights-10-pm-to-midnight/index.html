The Strait, AM 1610 The Station, Detroit Electronic Vinyl on Saturday nights 10 pm to midnight

Mar 16, 2013

One of my favorite radio shows is from an utterly obscure low power AM radio station in Hamtramck, which puts out an ultra lofi low power over-the-air signal on AM 1610 and a digital broadcast on http://am1610.org . The show is "The Strait", which features Detroit electronic vinyl. It's on Saturday nights from 10:00 p.m. to midnight, and it's always worth a listen. 

Some day I'll get to Hamtramck and do an air check and make sure I can actually tune this in - I don't know if they cover the whole city or just a piece of it.
