Paw Paws at Cobblestone Farm Market on Tuesday, 22 October 2013

Paw Paws at Cobblestone Farm Market on Tuesday, 22 October 2013

The following was posted to the Cobblestone Farm mailing list and is repeated here for your edification (and enjoyment of distinctive fruit).

Cobblestone Market has much to offer you and your family this Tuesday evening (4pm-7pm). Come try some new tastes, then warm your hands and feet by our fire pit!  

There will be paw paws at the market tomorrow, thanks to Marc Boone and his volunteers! Paw paws are a native fruit which tastes a like a banana and mango combined.  Come have a taste and take some home. For more information on paw paws, see  http://www.livescience.com/34669-what-is-a-paw-paw.html 

Also for sampling, will be Locavorious with frozen local fruits & veggies. Welcome back,Locavorious! http://www.locavorious.com/

Lydia and Mum are excited to share their new baking adventures which will include Winter Squash and Pumpkin pies (even a gluten free pie), breads, whole wheat pumpkin muffins along with expanding choices of whole grain, gluten free, and vegan breads.   

Besides all this will be the usual at market: apples, winter squash, freshly dug potatoes, broccoli, giant kohlrabi suitable for winter storage ,eggplant, beets, carrots, salad greens, collards, kale, onions, tomatoes, garlic, peppers, cabbage, chard, celery, herbs, turnips, Brussels sprouts.  Also at the market: goat milk soap, jams & jellies, grass-fed beef and pork, honey, eggs, artisan bread, baked goods, artisan bars, flowers dinner food, plants, tool sharpening, chair messages and more! Herbology Organics, which had to cancel at the last minute last week, plans to be there with candles, lotion and other skin care products including soaps, balms and oils.  

10/22/13 Events:

4-4:30 pm Kids Crafts with Stacey and Starr

4:30-5 pm Weekly Kids Rhythm Circle

5-6 pm Giant Bubbles with Jeanne and Pattie & Music by Lawrence Miller the Accordion Piper

6-7 pm Music by Samantha Steinbacher, Indie Singer/Songwriter

 

If you have food or toiletries that you would like to donate to Back Door Food Pantry (http://www.backdoorfoodpantry.org/), Cobblestone Farm Market is setting aside a donation box at the information table each week.  Please take a look at their website for items needed. 

 

See you at the market,

 

Jackie and Cobblestone Farm Market Team
