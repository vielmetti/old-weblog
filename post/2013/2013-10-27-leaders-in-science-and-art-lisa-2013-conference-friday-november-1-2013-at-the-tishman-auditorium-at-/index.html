Leaders in Science and Art (LISA 2013) is a conference bringing together technology and the arts in New York City on Friday, November 1, 2013. From the LISA 2013 conference program:

On November 1, 2013, 40 of the worldâ€™s top experts in digital and interactive art installation, data visualization, software art, interactive advertising, 3D printing, electronic sculpture, LED design, projection mapping, mobile art, tech wearables, app development, open source art, augmented reality, hacker art, new media curating, and digital art collecting will gather on stage at the Tishman Auditorium at The New School.
