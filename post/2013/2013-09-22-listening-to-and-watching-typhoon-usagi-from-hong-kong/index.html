Listening to and watching Typhoon Usagi from Hong Kong

Listening to and watching Typhoon Usagi from Hong Kong

As seen on GlobalTuners.com from VR2HF -

Should be some interesting listening on the aviation frequencies in the next 48 hours as Typhoon Usagi makes a direct hit on Hong Kong. I will go my best to keep the receiver on during the storm. You can find updated weather and weather radar at the Hong Kong Observatory - US Navy Forecast.

Dan Van Hoy's personal weather station, mrdan.com, has real time weather, and his Hong Kong DX receiver tunes in AM, FM, aviation, and shortwave bands through Global Tuners.

A snapshot of the typhoon path here from 2pm EST Sunday 22 September 2013.
