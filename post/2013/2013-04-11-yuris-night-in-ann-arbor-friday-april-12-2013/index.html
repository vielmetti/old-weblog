Yuri's Night in Ann Arbor, Friday April 12, 2013

From the a2geeks mailing list - added links are mine.

Friday April 12th is Yuri's Night - celebrating spaceflight on the anniversary of Yuri Gagarin's flight in 1961, and the first space shuttle flight in 1981. The Ann Arbor District Library is bringing laptops running Kerbal Space Program (an awesomely fun space exploration sandbox game) to The Bar at 327 Braun Court. Come celebrate space exploration!
