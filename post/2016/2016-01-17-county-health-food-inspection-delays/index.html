Washtenaw County Health Department delays food inspection reporting from November 2015

Sharing this message from the Washtenaw County Health Department regarding their delayed publication of restaurant inspection reports.


Angela Parsons
12/22/15

Greetings all,

Sincere apologies, but we are experiencing technical difficulties
with our inspection software and I am unable to retrieve the
restaurant inspection list for November. Kristen is currently on
vacation and will return on December 29th. I am hoping that she
will be able to remedy the issue and will be able to send along the
results at that time.

So sorry for any inconvenience!!

Angie
