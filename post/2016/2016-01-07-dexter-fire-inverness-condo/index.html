Fire on Inverness in Dexter, Michigan

A condo on Inverness in Dexter, Michigan was on fire on Thursday evening, January 7, 2016. A first report came in at about 7:24 p.m. via MEI Dispatch. Dexter Area Fire Chief Robert Smith said no one was injured in the fire, according to MLive. Reports were monitored on Twitter, Facebook, and the Washtenaw County scanner on Broadcastify.

At least five fire departments - Scio Township, Putnam Township, Chelsea, Ann Arbor Township and Ann Arbor City - came to the aid of Dexter in fighting the blaze, according to reports monitored on Facebook by MEI Dispatch. MLive reported that Hamburg Township also assisted.

MLive reporter Darcie Moran and photographer Melanie Maxwell are at the scene and have filed this story, Fire tears through Dexter condo.

Matt King, 23, was finishing up dinner in his ground-level condo on Jan. 7 in the 4500 block of Inverness Street in the Huron Farms development when he heard “explosions” in the building.

A photo from the scene was provided by ABC 7 (Washington DC) meteorologist Steve Rudin on Twitter.
