saturday: menu plan, Used Cars, 1880s ann arbor panorama

The menu plan for tonight for the late supper for the resident scholar: spring salad w/asparagus, oyster mushrooms from Alpena, eggs from Grandma's Kitchen, spinach from Brines Farm, savory tofu from the coop, and a curry dressing. The boys will probably get something a lot simpler.

I wish I had rhubarb to have made stewed rhubarb but I got there to market too late.  A stewed rhubarb is a compote, which is not to say a compost.  Don't eat the leaves, they have oxalic acid which is toxic.
