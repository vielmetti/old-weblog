Alice Kim, former third grade teacher at Burns Park Elementary School, passed away on Sunday, Sept. 20, 2009

from the AnnArbor.com obituaries:

Mrs. Alice Kim passed away on Sunday September 20, 2009. She was a third grade teacher at Burns Park Elementary School in Ann Arbor, MI for many years. She dedicated her life to her profession because she loved children and teaching. She is survived by her daughters and husband. She will be greatly missed by all of the people she taught, worked with, and loved. A private family funeral is planned. In lieu of flowers, a donation to the following would be preferred: "The University of Michigan" -Address: Department of Internal Medicine Development. 301 E. Liberty, Suite 400, Ann Arbor, MI 48104. Memo: Swartz Professorship in honor of Alice Kim.
