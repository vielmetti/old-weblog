What I would be doing if I were being incredibly attentive to your email

What I would be doing if I were being incredibly attentive to your email

Add you to my address book, and note carefully your telephone number and postal address.

Give you a call; thank you for your mail; ask some questions that came up as a result of the mail that were too complicated to answer in a followup email.

Send you a handwritten note as a follow up to the telephone call.

Locate you on culturally appropriate online social networks (not all of them, but which ever were sensible) and do the appropriate friending rituals; note any mutual friends.   Find someone who would be appropriate to make an introduction to, and make that connection.

Add some future related event to some appropriate calendar, to be reminded of it; note some future event to remind you of that may be of interest. 

Invite you to lunch; to coffee some time; to come over for tea some weekend.

Ask about your kids, your parents, your cat, your dog, your sweetie.
