Jon Udell and Andrew Turner talking about neogeography

May 3, 2009

Two friends, Jon Udell and Andrew Turner, in this piece on IT Conversations:

Neocartography, neogeography, the geospatial web: These words and phrases evoke the unfolding drama at the intersection of physical and virtual space. Andrew Turner is in the thick of the action. In this conversation with host Jon Udell he discusses GeoCommons, pocketmaps, collaborative data curation, and the evolution of design in a world of networked spatial and temporal data.


some more things to keep track of:

  • @ajturner and @judell on Twitter;
  • the new GeoRSS wiki; 
  • pocketmaps from kml files
  • the Vancouver (BC) Sun's parking ticket database visualized.
  • Andrew's blog at High Earth Orbit

it was the kind of interview that made me want to have a parallel "URL track" that would launch the pages were being referenced as they were referenced; I wonder if there's any closed captioning standard that would be useful for that task.
