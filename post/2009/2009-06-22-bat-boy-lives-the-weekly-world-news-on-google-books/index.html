Bat Boy Lives: The Weekly World News on Google Books

Bat Boy Lives: The Weekly World News on Google Books

Happily for the process of scholarly research, a good sized run of the Weekly World News has been digitized and is up on Google Books.


The New York Post reported in 2008 that Bat Boy LLC was going to take over publication of the Weekly World News, and sure enough there’s a fresh web site at weeklywordnews.com .
