Haiku for a construction project

The bridges are out
So I will go two new ways
Down your one way street

A bridge to nowhere
Barriers upend us all
Together we cope

Confusion arrives
Cars become pedestrians
Can't wait for State Street!

Stadium is closed?
The bridge is being torn down?
How do I get home?
