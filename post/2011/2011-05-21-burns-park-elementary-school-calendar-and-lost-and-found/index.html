Burns Park Elementary School calendar and lost and found

May 21, 2011

A convenient calendar for the end of the school year, which is to say that I’m doing this for next year.

I’ve been making a point when I visit the school to pick up or drop off J. to make a pass past the lost and found and put anything on a hanger that could possibly go onto a hanger. It’s impossible to know if that makes an immediate difference, but it’s satisfying to find a 5 minute volunteer opportunity that I can see.
