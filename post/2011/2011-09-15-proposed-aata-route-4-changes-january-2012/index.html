Proposed AATA Route 4 changes for January 2012 to get across campus faster

Sep 15, 2011

This route map, included in a recent AATA board packet, describes a new Route 4 routing which will speed up service to Ypsilanti by serving the U of Michigan Central Campus Transit Center instead of UM Mott Hospital on some routes. This is not a final map, and details of schedules were not in the packet.
