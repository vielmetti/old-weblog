Grand Rapids, MI shootings: coverage

As of 9:30 p.m., 7 are dead in shootings in Grand Rapids. The suspect, 34-year-old Rodrick Shonte Dantzler, has hostages. I'm not going to try to keep up with the news, but here are some links to follow.

UPDATE: at 11:50 p.m. the hostage situation is over, and the suspect is dead with a self-inflicted gunshot wound.

Audio: Radio Reference has the Kent County scanner running live; at the moment there are over 6000 people listening in. Scanner traffic is quite intense.

Video: WZZM-TV (ABC 13) is running a live stream of their coverage. Twitter: @wzzm13

Live blog: The Grand Rapids Press live blog links to the series of stories they have published. Twitter: @grpress

Twitter: Grand Rapids is trending and has a mix of news and reaction.
