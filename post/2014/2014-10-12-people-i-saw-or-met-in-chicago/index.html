People I saw or met in Chicago

Just a list, so I can't forget who I saw on a very good trip to Chicago last week.

  • Megabus. Cheap and cheerful transportation, very convenient to where I was going, good drivers both ways. The return trip bus was kind of old (e.g. the power outlets didn't work) but the driver was fantastic.
  • HI-Chicago a hostel in the loop. Cheap and cheerful lodging, very convenient.
  • Eric Sinclair and Anne Zender.
