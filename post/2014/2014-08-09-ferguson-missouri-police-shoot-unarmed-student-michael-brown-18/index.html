Ferguson, Missouri police shoot unarmed student Michael Brown, 18

Who: Michael Brown, age 18.

What: Shot by Ferguson, Missouri police.

When: Brown was walking, unarmed, at 2:15 p.m. on Saturday, August 19, 2014.

Where: Ferguson, MO is 20 minutes northwest of St. Louis, MO.

Why: Unknown. The unnamed officer has been put on paid leave, and the St. Louis County, MO Police will investigate.

Media:

KMOV 4, St Louis. Heavy police presence in Ferguson after fatal officer-involved shooting.

According to Stark, there are at least 100 police cars and numerous officers armed with M-16's in the area. Stark also says hundreds of people have gathered at the intersection of West Florissant Avenue and Canfield Drive to protest the incident. A large police force from 15 different departments responded, including riot-control officers.
