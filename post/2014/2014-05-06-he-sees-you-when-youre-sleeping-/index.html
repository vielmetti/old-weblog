He sees you when you're sleeping

6 May 2014

He sees you when you're sleeping

He sees you when you're sleeping
He knows when you're awake
He knows if you've been bad or good
So be good for goodness sake

The startup Buffer has a radical approach to transparency. Corporate minders watch your every move, on and off the clock, and the results of their nosy prying are available to all of your coworkers. From Inc. -

We recently gave everyone at Buffer a Jawbone UP wristband. It allows you to automatically track your sleep, your daily steps, your nutrition, and a lot more.

One of our key values at Buffer is to work smarter, not harder. Personal improvement is a big part of that, so giving employees a tool that can help improve their sleep patterns is a no-brainer.

A few weeks in it's already had an incredible effect. Browsing everyone's sleep patterns and talking about how to get more deep sleep has an amazing effect on productivity.

It's good when companies give off a clear "this is not for me" signal.
