September 22, 2014 Ann Arbor Civic Technology meetup, and a2civictech Github issues repo

The RSVP for the Ann Arbor Civic Technology Meetup has been posted. The event is September 22, 2014 at 7:00 p.m. and will be held at the Washtenaw County Building at 200 N Main St in Ann Arbor in the lower level.

If you want some action earlier than 9/22 and can't wait to get your civics on, you can join in to the action on Twitter by following the #a2council tag and coming to the Monday September 15, 2014 Ann Arbor City Council meeting. There's an agenda available, copied in from the original in Legistar and then annotated, for your meeting preparation pleasure.

I've also reactivated the a2civictech Github repo and made a suggested issues list to track some of the tech related issues that might be suitable for activism or public commentary or quiet conversations with staff and elected officials. The issues identified range everywhere from the mundane (locating places for a2civictech to meet) to the grandiose (reforming civic copyright on maps) with all manner of stuff in between.
