Did you feel it? M6.0 earthquake, 6km nw of American Canyon, Napa County CA

At 3:20 a.m. Pacific Time, an earthquake estimated at magnitude 6.0 struck near American Canyon, Napa County, California.

The USGS event page says more.

Scanner traffic: Napa County Fire (1300+ listeners), Vallejo Police and Fire (450+ listeners).

As usual I found out about this event on Twitter. The hashtag #quake is trending.
