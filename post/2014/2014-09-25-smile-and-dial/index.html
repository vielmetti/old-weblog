smile and dial

Sep 25, 2014

My address book fails me, in that it does not have any same way to encode the best way to reach someone.

Should I just call? Is it best to confirm that you are not busy now? Do you so instant message, Twitter DM, some other single purpose system that you are always available on? Should I revert to email to schedule something for later?

It is always appropriate to send a postcard, but I have not figured out how to make that an instant process, not that it really wants to be.

Edward Vielmetti
