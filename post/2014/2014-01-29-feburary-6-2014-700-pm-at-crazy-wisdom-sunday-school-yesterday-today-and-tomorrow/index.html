29 January 2014

Feburary 6, 2014, 7:00 p.m. at Crazy Wisdom: Sunday School: Yesterday, Today, and Tomorrow.

Join interfaith minister [Lauren Zinn] at SUNDAY SCHOOL: YESTERDAY, TODAY & TOMORROW for an interesting and energizing conversation about what's happening, not happening and will be happening in Sunday Schools, and why you might want to know.

Encourage religious school teachers, clergy, intermarried friends, parents of young children, students, etc. to attend this open conversation initiated by several local Sunday School teachers involved in interfaith education for youth.

See you there, with a cup of hot tea!

Crazy Wisdom SALON: Inspiring Talks in the Tea Room, February 6, 2014 at 7:00 p.m.

Crazy Wisdom Bookstore and Tea Room, 114 S Main St, Ann Arbor, MI 48104

Store phone number: (734) 665-2757

  • Greg Briggs is Associate Pastor at Bethlehem United Church of Christ in Ann Arbor where he directs the youth programming. He is interested in using pop culture to promote social justice.
  • Kellie Love has been co-teaching for the Youth Ed program at the Interfaith Center for Spiritual Growth for 10 years.
  • Heide Kaminski taught the Youth Ed program for 10 years at Unity of Ann Arbor and for the past 12 years at the Interfaith Center. She considers herself eclectic with a family of agnostics, atheists, and traditional Christians.
  • Lauren Zinn of ZinnHouse, Educational Planning PhD & Interfaith Minister, runs an independent Interfaith Orientation and Hebrew program for all ages, and trains future teachers in Teaching Interfaith to Youth.
