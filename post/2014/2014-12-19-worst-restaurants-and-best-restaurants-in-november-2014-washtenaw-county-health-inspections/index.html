Worst restaurants (and best restaurants) in November 2014 Washtenaw County health inspections

Every month I get an email with a spreadsheet of restaurant inspection reports from Washtenaw County. Here's a summary of the worst (and best) of the restaurants inspected in November 2014.

There are three types of problems reported by county health. To simplify reporting I score them proportionately to see which locations go to the top of the list.

6 locations had no problems at all:

  • DOG DAYS III 2515 JACKSON ANN ARBOR
  • DOMINICK'S 812 MONROE ANN ARBOR
  • HOTEL HICKMAN WILD WEST ADVENTURES STFU 2400 HICKMAN RD ANN ARBOR
  • JIMMY JOHN'S GOURMET SANDWICHES 342 S. STATE STREET ANN ARBOR
  • OCED SENIOR NUTRITION PROGRAM 2025 E. CLARK ROAD YPSILANTI
  • WETZEL'S PRETZELS 100 BRIARWOOD CIRCLE K108 ANN ARBOR

By far and away the worst restaurant is Tim Horton's on 760 S Hewitt Rd in Ypsilanti, with 9 priority violations, 8 priority foundation violations, and 14 core violations. This sums it up (all caps in original)

ACTIVE MANAGERIAL CONTROL IS NOT BEING DEMONSTRATED IN THE FACILITY AS EVIDENCED BY EMPLOYEES ARE PROPERLY HANDWASHING, EMPLOYEES ARE MONITORING FOOD TEMPERATURES, PROPERLY WASHING AND SANITIZING CLEANED MULTIUSE EQUIPMENT AND UTENSILS BEFORE THEY ARE REUSED, MONITORING DISHWASHING PROCEDURES. BASED ON NUMEROUS VIOLATIONS OCCURRING WITHIN THESE FOOD CODE CATEGORIES ACTIVE MANAGERIAL CONTROL IS NOT OCCURRING ON A REGULAR BASIS. CORRECT BY RETRAIN MANAGERS, EMPLOYEES TO BE IN COMPLIANCE WITH FOOD CODE AND PROVIDE TOOLS FOR MONITORING CLEANING, TEMPERATURES ETC FOR MANAGER TO BE ABLE TO MAINTAIN COMPLIANCE. ,

The Yelp reviews for this place share some of the horrors.

Bill S.:

Disgusting - worst sandwich I have ever purchased in my life. Dried out chicken that tasted like it bad been sitting around for 3 days. Threw it out after 2 bites. Would have asked for a refund but didn't want to deal with the aggravated and stressed out staff running the place.

Mia M:

Usually, the same person is running the drive thru window. She's rude and I overhear conversations that make me question the cleanliness inside the restaurant. "Grab a wet rag and pretend you wiped down the tables." "Put a towel on it and we'll clean it up later." Or my personal favorite, "We're out of coffee [and it's 8 o'clock in the morning.]"

Want to know how your favorite restaurant scored? See Sword Solutions which has the restaurant inspection database. Sadly there's no way to link directly to an individual report.
