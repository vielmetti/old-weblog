Chickpea, Potato and Tomato Stew: six recipes to mix and match from

Haven't cooked any of these yet, but there's some recipes to work through:

Ricki Heller: Chickpea, Potato and Tomato Stew. Uses tahini to enrich the sauce.

Fit Pregnancy: Chickpea, Potato and Tomato Stew with Chard . With chard, paprika, parsley.

The First Mess: moroccan-style vegetable + chickpea stew . Sweet potatoes instead of potatoes, and a cinnamon+coriander+cumin spice.

The Picky Eater: Indian Chickpea and Potato Stew. Curry is the flavor here, and cilantro replaces parsley.

Food and Wine: New Potato, Chickpea and Tomato Stew. The surprise ingredient is chorizo.

Naturally Ella: Sweet Potato and Chickpea Stew with Quinoa. Quinoa is the novel grain.
