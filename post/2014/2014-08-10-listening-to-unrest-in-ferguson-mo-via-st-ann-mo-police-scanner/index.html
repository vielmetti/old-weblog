Listening to unrest in Ferguson, MO via Twitter, police and fire scanner, Hot 104.1, KTVI TV

August 10-11, 2014: It's a tense night in Ferguson, MO after last night's shooting of 18 year old Michael Brown.

News account from the St Louis Post Dispatch, with photos: Violence breaks out near site of vigil for teen killed by police.

FERGUSON â€¢ A day of protests and vigils Sunday for an unarmed black teenager who was shot to death by a Ferguson police officer erupted Sunday night with confrontations, looting and gunshots.

To follow what's happening:

Scanner: The St. Ann Police scanner feed has police activity. Canine units, riot gear being staged at a Target at this writing (21:40 Central Time). Scanner feed offline (23:06 CT). Broadcastify has St Louis County scanner feeds from multiple agencies. N St Louis County Fire and EMS online (23:15 CT).

Twitter: St Louis alderman Antonio French is on the scene with measured commentary. He's also using Vine to provide frequent brief video clips from the scene.

Twitter: Patricia Bynes, @patricialicious, Democratic Committeewoman of Ferguson Township in St Louis County in MO.

Twitter: Brittany Noble, @BrittanyNoble, KMOV reporter.

Twitter: The #ferguson hashtag, monitored via tchat.io: http://www.tchat.io/rooms/ferguson monitored via the web app: https://twitter.com/hashtag/ferguson.

News media: KTVI Live, Fox 2 News. Live news stream via a cell phone camera of the scene.

News media: Hot 104.1 is the St Louis MO hip hop station that has been covering the situation from the perspective of the community. Twitter at @Hot1041.
