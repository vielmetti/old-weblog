Where do you write something that's longer than a tweet, but shorter than an essay?

Where do you write something that's longer than a tweet, but shorter than an essay?

There's a space on the net for things that you put together that don't fit into a tweet, but that also don't really require a whole long essay.

I've been writing these in my blog for years, and so I have a bit of infrastructure put together for organizing, collecting, and finding them again. A lot of people who are on Twitter don't have a blog, though, and so their fragments stay fragmentary or get lost in the mists of Twitter time.

There are plenty of places to post disconnected paragraphs online - you can stick them on Facebook (where they will be quickly seen and quickly lost), you can dump them into a Github gist (where only techies will be able to find them again), or - best of all - you can use your own web space in some blog-like time-sequenced away to push things into the world for later retrieval.

Some other people talking about this at the moment:

  • Gina Trapani: I find tweets too reductionist and Medium pieces too bloviating
  • Marco Arment: Too much of my writing in the last few years has gone exclusively into Twitter. I need to find a better balance.
  • Andy Baio: I miss seeing people I admire post stuff longer than a tweet.

It was noted by several people that Tumblr fits this pattern well; alas, I don't Tumbl very well.
