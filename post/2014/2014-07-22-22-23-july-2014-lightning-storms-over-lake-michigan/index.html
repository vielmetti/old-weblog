22-23 July 2014 Lightning storms over Lake Michigan

This map from LightningMaps.ORG shows lightning over Lake Michigan and into Ottawa County, Michigan at about 11:15 p.m on 22 July 2014. Note the strong line of storms crossing the lake.


The corresponding power outage map is from Consumers Energy. Outage reporting generally lags behind realtime by 15-30 minutes, and of course not every lightning strike turns into a power problem (though sometimes it seems like it does).

Ottawa County, Michigan radio feeds are available on Broadcastify.

It appears that the SKYWARN repeater's internet feed (147.060 K8DAA repeater, owned by the Holland Amateur Radio Club in Holland, Michigan) is offline. Look up K8DAA on Repeaterbook to get contact details. "Membership in the Holland Amateur Radio Club is open to any and all persons who have an interest in amateur radio."
