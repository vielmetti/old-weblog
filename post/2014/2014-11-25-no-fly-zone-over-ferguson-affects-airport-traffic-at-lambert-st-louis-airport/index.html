No fly zone over Ferguson affects airport traffic at Lambert-St Louis Airport

Nov 25, 2014

November 25, 2014, midnight.

FAA has issued TFR affecting inbound flights. Airport is still open. Does not impact departures.

— Lambert-STL Airport (@flystl) November 25, 2014

FAA TFR, NOTAM : 4/0271 Location : FERGUSON, MO, Tuesday, November 25, 2014 UTC. Not for navigation.


Flightstats shows that traffic is being diverted from the airport.


The FAA has issued a TFR over #Ferguson. But unlike last time, commercial flights are diverting away from @flystl. pic.twitter.com/NL462dKAdg
