Solar panel + batteries available for checkout at Ann Arbor District Library

Oct 14, 2014

I checked out a 27 watt solar panel with a very beefy battery from the Ann Arbor District Library. The battery also charges via a wall wart, and provides either 5V USB power or switchable 10V-19V DC.

I've been thinking about getting a solar panel, and my conclusion is that 27 watts is overkill for my normal use case which is powering my phone. The battery was awesome, but again overkill - too heavy to be an everyday carry.

My next checkout will be this kit with a 7 watt panel that looks more portable.

Power to the people!
