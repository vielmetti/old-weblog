I'm taking a break from Twitter

30 November 2014

I'm taking a break from Twitter

Nothing personal, but it's time to take a break from Twitter.

I tried to do this back in 2008 when it was still possible to have a reasonable conversation on a blog about whether you were or weren't going to participate in a new social media experiment. Nowadays Twitter is big business, and I don't feel like I have to justify myself to my friends for why I think it's time to take a rest from 24x7 news and celebrity updates.

I have a few bots that tweet out weather updates which I guess I will keep running because it's more trouble than it's worth to temporarily turn them off.
