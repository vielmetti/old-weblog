Discardia is September 22 and 23, 2014

Discardia is a quarterly holiday that helps you get rid of things. This year's fall season dates are September 22 (the equinox) to September 23 (the new moon), 2014. I'm looking forward at the very least to a cleanout of the basement, which as my son points out is full of 'crap from the 1990s'.

See www.discardia.com for details; the origin of the event is due to Dinah Sanders.
