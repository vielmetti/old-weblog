Run away and join the Circus, for just one night. Nov 7, 2014 fundraiser for FLY Art Center


On Friday, Nov. 7, 2014, FLY Art Center's annual Circus fundraiser will create a circus, with a menagerie of carnival games and exotic performers in the Bona Sera underground space, 200 W. Michigan Ave., Ypsilanti, MI. Music and mingling, cash bar and circus food as well as Bona Sera's inventive and eclectic menu times. 6:30 to 10 pm. Cost: Donation of at least 25 cents.

While the adults play at the Circus, children are invited to their own fun time at the Circus Side Show! Eat pizza, learn about the artist Alexander Calder and make your own cool moving sculpture to take home! 6-8 p.m. FLY Art Center, 40 N. Huron St., Ypsilanti. Kids event is free to Circus attendees. Must pre-register, space is limited to 12 children.

For more information go to http://www.flyartcenter.org/flying-circus/

To register for the free Kids event go to: http://flyartcenter.wildapricot.org/event-1778449
