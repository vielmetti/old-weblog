Sayre Fire, Los Angeles

15 November 2008

Sayre Fire, Los Angeles

Hot on the heels of the Tea Fire comes word of another big fire in Los Angeles, the Sayre Fire.

Here's the news sources I was able to round up quickly; no doubt this will be changing fast.

LAFD suggests these social media tags and channels:

  • Twitter hashtag: #LAFIRE
  • Flickr tags: "SayreFire" and "LAFD" and add to LAFD Photo Pool
  • YouTube tags: "SayreFire" and "LAFD"
  • Los Angeles Fire Department: Fire update 6am Sat PST; Fire evacuation map;
  • Los Angeles DWP: Major power lines taken out of service
  • Blog: LAist - power shut off by DWP;  freeway closure map;
  • Newspaper: LA Times - overview with map and videos
  • Television: KABC 7 live video
  • Radio: KNX 1070 CBS news radio
  • Hospital: Olive View Hospital in path of fire (LA Times)
  • Metrolink: Antelope Valley Saturday service cancelled
  • Twitter: following LAist, Metrolink, LAFD

I'm particularly looking for news about Reseda (family in the area).
