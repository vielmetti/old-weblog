Green beans with tomatoes and lemon juice and no white wine

The other name of this recipe is "fried last week's vegetables".  It turned out pretty well.

green beans
tomatoes
oil
garlic
dried basil
lemon juice
no white wine before noon on Sunday

Wash and trim the green beans, chop the tomatoes, smash the garlic.

Heat the oil in a big pan, saute the garlic, add the green beans, add the tomatoes.  Sprinkle on dried basil.

At some point the tomatoes will cook enough that the bottom of the pan will start to have browned tomatoey bits.  At that point start to deglaze the pan.  I used lemon juice and a little water, but it would have been at least as good with white wine.

Why didn't I have white wine?  Because the weekly shopping run is on Sunday mornings, and Michigan has a ban on Sunday morning alcohol sales (until noon).  In 2006, House Bill 6354 would have repealed this restriction.

I don't know how to navigate the entire Michigan legislative system, but next time some bill like that comes up I'd like to know so I can encourage it passing.
