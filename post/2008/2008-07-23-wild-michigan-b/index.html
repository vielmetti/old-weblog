Wild Michigan Blueberries - a photo album (w/many comments)

Webshots contributor "candistackable" has a photo album of Michigan wild blueberry picking from 2004.

In our part of Michigan (Topinabee) the woods are thick with wild blueberries toward the end of July and the beginning of August. I hope you enjoy my images.

There's lots of pictures, and lots of comments on the pictures, including this one from "jrgoflo":

I loved these pictures. There's really nothing better than Wild Michigan blueberries. My favorite place to pick them is at the Shelldrake Dam State Forest Campground NW of Paradise in the U.P. or the Betsy Lake area of Tahquamenon Falls State Park. I've just planted 10 lowbush blueberry shrubs at my house. Can't wait!!

I've asked for permission to reuse a photo (and will add one here if I get that).
