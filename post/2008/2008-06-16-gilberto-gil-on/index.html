Gilberto Gil on Digital Music at AADL, Tues June 17 2008

16 June 2008

Gilberto Gil on Digital Music at AADL, Tues June 17 2008

as seen on a umich mailing list:


In conjunction with his June 18 concert at the Ann Arbor Summer Festival, Brazilian superstar Gilberto Gil will participate in a panel discussion on Digital Music, Copyright and Internet Rights at the Downtown Library on Tues., June 17 at 7 pm. The famed tropicalist musician serves as Brazil's Minister of Culture. W. Kim Heron, editor of the Metro Times, will moderate the discussion.

In addition to Mr. Gil, panelists include: Prof. Mark Clague, Assoc. Director of the UM School of Music, Theatre & Dance's American Music Institute; Jessica Litman, expert on copyright law, author of Digital Copyright (2001), and Professor of Law at the UM Law School; and Christopher M. Taylor, a shareholder practicing in Butzel Long's Ann Arbor office in the areas of media law, intellectual property, technology and e-commerce.
