It's getting to be that time of year when the streets are crowded with eager fans looking for a place to park for Michigan football. 

Here's a list of blogs covering the 2008 season - not complete and not in any order - for reference.

Stadium and Main has notes about Comcast and the Big Ten Network in other cities.

The Hoover Street Rag has the band program schedule for the year.
