November 2, 2008: Ann Arbor Active Against ALS Family Field Day

Family Field Day, to benefit Ann Arbor Active Against ALS*, is THIS COMING SUNDAY, November 2nd, at Burns Park, from 1-4pm. Registration forms and a drop box are in the office, or you can email a2a3als@gmail.com, to let us know you're coming.

Early in the afternoon we will have relay races, a scavenger hunt and kickball game for adults and kids of all ages. From 3-4, some of Ann Arbor's best coaches are running clinics - field hockey, football, lacrosse and soccer. Your child will be able to participate in any clinic of his/her choice.

There will be brats for sale, as well as baked goods, Caribou coffee, hot chocolate and cider, t-shirts, and books!
Please help us plan for the day - register NOW!

Suggested donation is $20 per family, and all proceeds benefit ALS research.
Thanks in advance for you support!
