Creating vs consuming

Did you ever wake up from a long session online and discover that you had very little to show for it? There's a pattern of internet use that's simple and very disturbingly unproductive. Go to you favorite network, read everything, get bored when the refresh doesn't produce anything new. Switch networks, repeat. When you've visited everything, go back to the first one and hope that there's something new. It's a wonderful way to consume content, and since you're always looking at new things it's a way to perpetually be entertained by novelty. »
