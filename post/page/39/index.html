LinkedIn has had a password leak. How to repair it.

Change your LinkedIn password. News emerged on Wednesday, June 6, 2012 that LinkedIn had a leak of its password database. You can protect yourself by changing your LinkedIn password, and by also changing any other passwords that you use which are the same. »

If the network were actually down, you could work with that, knowing that eventually it would be a time and place that you could reconnect.
