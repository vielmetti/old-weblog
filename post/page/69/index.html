Green beans with tomatoes and lemon juice and no white wine

The other name of this recipe is "fried last week's vegetables".  It turned out pretty well. green beans tomatoes oil garlic dried basil lemon juice no white wine before noon on Sunday Wash and trim the green beans, chop the tomatoes, smash the garlic. Heat the oil in a big pan, saute the garlic, add the green beans, add the tomatoes.  Sprinkle on dried basil. At some point the tomatoes will cook enough that the bottom of the pan will start to have browned tomatoey bits. »

Radish salad with soy and sesame dressing

This is the simplest possible thing to make with a bunch of radishes fresh from farmers market ($1.25 this week). one bunch radishes vinegar soy sauce sesame oil Wash and trim the radishes.  Slice thin, put in a bowl.  Add vinegar (I used white vinegar, but rice vinegar would do well), soy sauce, and a little sesame oil; you're after enough to moisten, not to cover.  Let sit a few minutes before eating. »
