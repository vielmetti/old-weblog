A light brown to dark brown in color, little browns are fairly small in size with a wingspan of 8 1⁄2 to 11 1⁄2 inches, small ears, and large feet. In summer, colonies of the species live in hot attics and under shingles and siding; in winter, they hibernate in caves, crevices, houses, hollow trees, or mines. »
